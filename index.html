Why this matters

Most sellers look at the price first. Buyers often hesitate for other reasons.

Low offers or quiet open homes do not always mean your property is a bad property. Often buyers give warning signs before they write an offer — fewer second viewings, vague feedback, concerns about condition, hesitation around documents, or comparing your home against stronger alternatives.

“The earlier you identify what buyers are reacting to, the more control you have before momentum is lost.”
